Course Title 1 - Fashion Designing

About the Course

This course offers comprehensive training in fashion designing, covering various aspects from basic sketching to advanced pattern making and garment construction. You will learn how to translate your ideas into tangible designs, understand the fundamentals of textiles, and develop skills to create your own fashion collections.

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Fashion Design
  • Sketching and Illustration
  • Textile Science
  • Pattern Making
  • Garment Construction
  • Fashion Marketing and Merchandising

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this course can pursue various career paths such as fashion designer, textile designer, fashion illustrator, pattern maker, and fashion merchandiser. The course also prepares you for entrepreneurship in the fashion industry.

Course Details

Duration: 6 months

Mode: Full-time / Part-time

Fee: $3000
